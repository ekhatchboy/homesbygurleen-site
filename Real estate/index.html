Recommended concierge behavior

  • Start with whether the lead is buying, selling, or both.
  • Ask one question at a time to keep the conversation natural.
  • Capture contact info before ending the chat.
  • Offer a showing, valuation, or consult as the main conversion goal.

Operational upgrades

  • Send every qualified lead to email, CRM, or Google Sheets.
  • Sync consultations to Calendly or Google Calendar.
  • Tag buyer vs seller and estimated timeline automatically.

Live agent settings

  • Set the realtor name, market, and contact email in backend environment variables.
  • Optionally add a `LEAD_WEBHOOK_URL` to forward qualified leads to a CRM or automation tool.
  • The assistant can work as a buyer intake, seller intake, or consultation concierge.
